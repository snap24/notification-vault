Notification Vault is a resilient, open-source notification logger designed for high security and 100% offline privacy. It seamlessly intercepts, records, and categorizes your device's notifications using a robust local database, guaranteeing that your personal data never leaves your device.

Key Features:
- Complete Privacy: Operates entirely offline with zero internet permissions.
- Biometric Lock: Enterprise-grade security enforcing fingerprint or face unlock before the app can be accessed.
- Search & Filter: Instantly locate past notifications with powerful text search and package-based filtering.
- Dynamic Theming: Supports multiple color schemes (Grey, Blue, Green, Orange, Purple) and a bespoke Pitch Black AMOLED mode.
- Material 3 Design: Clean, modern interface with beautiful animations and a pristine user onboarding experience.
